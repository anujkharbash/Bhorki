About Bhorki

Bhorki is a Village in Udaipurwati Tehsil in Jhunjhunu District of Rajasthan State, India. It belongs to Jaipur Division . It is located 34 KM towards South from District head quarters Jhunjhunu. 12 KM from Udaipurwati. 130 KM from State capital Jaipur
Bhorki Pin code is 333032 and postal head office is Bhorki .
This Place is in the border of the Jhunjhunu District and Sikar District. Sikar District Khandela is South towards this place .

Dhamora ( 5 KM ) , Jakhal ( 5 KM ) , Raghunathpura ( 6 KM ) , Gudha Gorji ( 6 KM ) , Duria ( 6 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Bhorki. Bhorki is surrounded by Nawalgarh Tehsil towards west , Jhunjhunun Tehsil towards North , Khetri Tehsil towards East , Khandela Tehsil towards South .
